## 8. FAQ (mirrors EngineerBabu's FAQ block — genuinely useful for objection-handling)

**Section tag:** FAQs
**Section title:** Questions before you reach out

**What services does NovaDesk actually offer?**
Full-stack web development (Next.js, FastAPI, Supabase) and AI integration (LLM features, RAG, automation). I intentionally focus on these rather than offering everything, so the quality stays high.

**How much does a project cost?**
Depends on scope. A simple landing page or MVP module can start around ₹15,000-30,000; a full-stack product with AI features typically runs higher. You'll get a clear, itemized quote after a free discovery call — no vague ballparks.

**How long does a typical project take?**
A focused MVP usually takes 2-4 weeks. Larger builds with multiple features run 6-10 weeks. You'll know the realistic timeline after the discovery call, not a guess upfront.

**Do I get to see progress during the build, or just the final result?**
You see working software every week — not a status update. That's non-negotiable in how I work.

**Will you personally build my project, or hand it off to someone else?**
Personally. NovaDesk is one person — the same person who scopes the project writes the code and deploys it.

**Do you sign NDAs?**
Yes, happy to sign an NDA before any detailed discussion if you need one.

**What happens after launch — do you disappear?**
No. Every project includes a defined support window post-launch for bug fixes and questions. Ongoing work beyond that is available separately if needed.

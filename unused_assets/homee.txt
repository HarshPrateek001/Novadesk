# NovaDesk — Complete Homepage Content & SEO Package

**Positioning used throughout:** Founder-led, boutique full-stack & AI development studio. Honest, specific claims only — no unverifiable numbers, no fake "featured in" logos. This is the single biggest fix from the current site: one consistent identity from meta tags to footer.

**Primary target keywords:** custom software development agency India, AI development studio, MVP development company India, full-stack development agency, SaaS development company India, hire software development agency (not "hire developers" — that keyword pulls staff-aug buyers, wrong intent for you).

## 4. HEADING STRUCTURE FOR SEO (H1 → H2 → H3)

Only **one H1** on the page (the hero headline). Everything else should be H2, with H3 for sub-cards. Current likely issue: verify "Built for founders...", "Why founders work directly...", "Find The Right Solutions For You", "You See Working Software Every Week", "Solo builder vs. a traditional agency", "Recognized For Building...", "Your Software Development Questions, Answered" are all H2 — not H1 or plain styled divs. Search engines weight H1/H2 significantly; a page with 7 H1s dilutes topical signal.
